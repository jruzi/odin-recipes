Raspberry Brownies

Description

I had no interest in making brownies until I tried a version of this recipe. The red veins running through the chocolate, complimenting it's flavor and contrasting it to the richness of the brownie became a favorite of mine in no time. I hope you will enjoy it as much as I do, especially if you aren't a chocolate lover - this recipe will be the devil's advocate of your palette.

Ingredients for Brownies

  • ½ cup butter
  • 225 grams semi sweet chocolate chips, about 1½ cups
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • ½ cup flour
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ⅛ tsp baking soda
  • ⅛ tsp baking powder

Ingredients for Raspberry Sauce

  • ½ cup frozen or fresh raspberries
  • ½ cup sugar
  • Small splash of water to get the sauce going
